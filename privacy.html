Privacy Policy

Cataloged is a personal reading tracker. We collect only the information necessary to create your account and store your library.

We use the Google Books API to help retrieve book titles and authors when adding books. No personal data is shared with Google.

Your data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising. We do not track you across websites and do not collect analytics beyond what is required for the app to function.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
